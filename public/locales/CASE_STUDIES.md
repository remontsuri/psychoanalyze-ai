# PsychoAnalyze AI - Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Anxious-Avoidant Attachment Analysis

**Client Profile:** 34-year-old woman, relationship difficulties

**Key Findings:**
- Primary Defense Mechanism: Intellectualization
- Emotional Pattern: High anxiety, withdrawal behaviors
- Attachment Style: Fearful-Avoidant (Disorganized)
- Risk Level: Medium

**Application:** Identified pattern of pursuing then withdrawing from partners, traced to childhood inconsistent caregiving.

## Case Study 2: Narcissistic Personality Traits Analysis

**Client Profile:** 45-year-old man, workplace conflicts

**Key Findings:**
- Primary Defense: Projection & Rationalization
- Emotional Triggers: Criticism and perceived rejection
- Theme: Power dynamics and control needs
- Academic Notes: Consistent with NPD criteria (DSM-5)

**Application:** Recognized grandiosity masking deep insecurity, used for targeted therapeutic approach.

---

## Case Study 3: Trauma Processing & PTSD

**Client Profile:** 28-year-old veteran with trauma history

**Key Findings:**
- Primary Defense: Dissociation
- Emotional Trajectory: Flat affect with sudden spikes
- Risk Level: High
- Therapy Recommendations: EMDR, somatic therapy

**Application:** Early identification of dissociative episodes enabled proactive intervention.

---

## Case Study 4: Depression with Rumination Patterns

**Client Profile:** 26-year-old student, academic performance decline

**Key Findings:**
- Emotional Pattern: Sustained sadness with guilt rumination
- Defense Mechanism: Self-blame and catastrophizing
- Sentiment Analysis: Predominantly negative with rare positive shifts
- Attachment: Anxious-Preoccupied

**Application:** Identified rumination cycles, designed metacognitive interventions.

---

## Case Study 5: Grief Processing & Adaptive Resilience

**Client Profile:** 52-year-old woman, loss of spouse

**Key Findings:**
- Emotional Progression: Denial -> Anger -> Acceptance
- Positive Coping: Strong support network utilization
- Growth Patterns: Post-traumatic growth indicators
- Resilience Factors: Strong sense of meaning

**Application:** Validated healthy grief trajectory, supported continued therapeutic work.

## Implementation Notes

Each case study demonstrates PsychoAnalyze AI's ability to:
1. Identify complex psychological patterns from conversational data
2. Detect emotional trajectories and turning points
3. Provide actionable therapeutic recommendations
4. Support clinical decision-making with AI-enhanced analysis
5. Track progress through quantifiable emotional metrics
